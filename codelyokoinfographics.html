Infographics: Code Lyoko

Dimensions: 11x14 in

Year Created: 2020



The Infographics shown were created for a school project, they were meant to show information about the French-animated show Code Lyoko, a show I grew fond of watching as a kid. These infographics were meant to show information about the plot of the show and the world within the show. The infographic discusses how many sectors there are in Lyoko, the virtual world within the show, the different costumes and the costume changes, and all of the entities or monsters that are seen as enemies or obstacles.

The fonts used in the posters were Gunship, Arial, and Times New Roman. These fonts were not used and will not be used for commercial use. The fonts used were used for personal use on these posters and I do not plan to make a profit from these fonts. Along with the fonts, logos and images from the show belong to the correct owners, ANTEFILMS and MoonScoop.
