Infographics: How To Make a 3D Origami Vase

The infographics shown here are about how to make a 3D Origami Vase. It's great for people who are beginners to make 3D Origami Figurines or the art of 3D Origami. 3D Origami has been a hobby of mine since high school and this infographic helps people who are interested in the art of 3D Origami create their own figurine. Making a 3D Origami Vase is one of the easiest figurines to make using just paper and an x-acto knife.
